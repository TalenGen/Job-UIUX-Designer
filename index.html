TalenGen Internship

Job Title: UIUX Designer

  • Location : In Person
  • Job Type: Internship
  • Compensation: Unpaid

About TalenGen:

TalenGen strives to be the #1 centralized platform in empowering the next generation of leaders in Southeast Asia through innovative recruitment and professional networking solutions. We aim to bridge the gap between students and employers by building a multilingual recruitment platform that offers multiple features such as AI matching, referral systems, and enhanced security to accommodate diverse cultures and identities.

Responsibilities:

  • Create wireframes, user flows, and prototypes using tools such as Figma and Canva to visualize and iterate on design concepts.
  • Test and gather feedback on prototypes to inform design improvements.
  • Develop visually appealing and consistent user interface designs.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers, developers, and marketers.
  • Ensure designs are responsive and optimized for various devices and screen sizes.

Qualifications:

  • Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer or similar role in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva, or similar.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to provide clear and concise project updates to the CEO, highlighting progress, achievements, and potential challenges.

Why Join Us:

  • Be part of a cutting-edge startup that is reshaping the student recruitment industry in Southeast Asia
  • Collaborate with a talented and diverse team of professionals.
  • Opportunities for career growth and professional development.
